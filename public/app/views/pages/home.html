Raising Accessibility

Dungeons & Dragons features a complex rule set, developed over the course of 40 years. It is multifaceted, and takes dedication to master. Tome brings pieces of the Dungeons & Dragons rule set into a comfortable and easy to navigate format.

Faster than Flipping

The Official Player's Handbook features 300+ content dense pages. Spells alone spread across 91; The growing list of official companion rule sets only increases this page count. By reformatting and reorganizing this wealth of arcane knowledge, players can spend less time with their noses' in the book, and more time with their heads' in the game.

Sharing is Caring
